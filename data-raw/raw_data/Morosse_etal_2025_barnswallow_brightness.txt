## TechnicalInfo

\# Data from: North American barn swallows pair, mate, and interact assortatively Dataset DOI: [10.5061/dryad.1g1jwsv8g](10.5061/dryad.1g1jwsv8g) ## Description of the data and file structure This dataset contains pairing, mating, and social interaction data for Morosse et al. 2025 Behavioral Ecology, a study on assortative behavior in North American barn swallows. In all cases, NA means there is missing data. 2022 3d edgelist MF.csv: Edgelist of male-female social interactions 2022 3d edgelist updated 10_30_23.csv: Edgelist of all social interactions FemaleWPEPcolorcompariso 08_07_24.csv: Datafile for comparing male plumage color between a female's social and extra-pair (EP) mates OH Dulog 2022 6_7_24 pruned.csv: Attribute file for birds in social network OH Dulog 2022 6_11_24 pruned for MF.csv: Attribute file for birds in the male-female social network SG color long 7_18_24.csv: Long format plumage color data for density plots SGMatingairs 08_07_24 final.csv: Mating pairs for mating assortativity analyses SGSocialPairs 7_7_24 for RS final.csv: Social pairs that have full reproductive success information SGSocialPairs 8_5_24 final.csv: Social pairs for assortativity analyses WPEPcolorcomparison 06_10_24.csv: Data file for comparing plumage color between birds who sired only within-pair (WP) young and those that sired extra-pair (EP) young.
