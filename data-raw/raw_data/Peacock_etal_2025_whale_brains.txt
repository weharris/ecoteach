# The evolution of hearing and brain size in Eocene whales

Dataset DOI: [10.5061/dryad.sf7m0cgh1](10.5061/dryad.sf7m0cgh1)

## Description of the data and file structure

Data on endocranial volume and body mass collected from various sources. With several new data points from CT scans and direct measurement of specimens.

### Files and variables

#### File: Description\_of\_H-GSP\_96314.docx (Zenodo)

**Description:** Description of jaw from juvenile pakicetid.

#### File: endocranial\_volume\_and\_body\_mass\_data\_for\_paleobiology.csv (Dryad)

**Description:** Endocranial volume and body mass data

##### Variables

* family: family level clade for each species
* binomial_name: full taxonomic name for each species
* common_name: a common name for each species
* endocranial_volume: endocranial volume in cubic centimetres 
* brain_mass: brain mass in grams
* ocw_mm: occipital condyle width in mm
* body_mass: body mass in kg
